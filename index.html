The World of Soccer

Soccer is considered as one of the most popular sports around the world. More than 200 million people participate in this sport. Although the sport is refered to soccer in the U.S., in many other countries, it's called "football". When playing a real game of soccer, it usually consists of 11 players on both teams, each player has their own position they're responsible for during the game. One of the cool thing's about soccer is that it can be played in many enviroments! Whether you want the traditional way of playing on a grassy pitch, or indoors, in your backyard, on the street, anyone can play however they want! With soccer already being a very popular sport, one of the biggest sporting events everyone around the world watches every 4 years is the Word Cup. This tournament brings in so many viewers, that the last World Cup in 2018, a total of 3.5 billion people all over the world tuned in to watch the games. This famous tournament consists of 32 teams representing their countries, it is defitnitely an event every sports fan and of course soccer fan anticipate every 4 years!
